Privacy Policy

Internet Speed Test is a free tool that respects your privacy. We do not collect personal information when you test your download speed, upload speed, or ping.

Last Updated: March 23, 2026
1

What This Site Does

Internet Speed Test is a free, static website that helps you check your internet connection speed. When you click "Start Test," all speed measurements — including download speed, upload speed, ping, and jitter — run entirely within your web browser. No test results are sent to or stored on our servers.

2

Information We Do Not Collect

Unlike many internet speed test websites, we keep things simple:

  • No account registration — there is no sign-up, login, or profile
  • No cookies — we do not set first-party or third-party cookies
  • No analytics tracking — no Google Analytics, no pixel tags, no fingerprinting
  • No advertising — no ad networks, no retargeting scripts
  • No speed test history — your results stay in your browser and are not saved

Zero Personal Data Collection

We built Internet Speed Test as a privacy-first alternative to ad-heavy speed test sites. Your connection data never leaves your device.

3

Web Hosting & Server Logs

This website is delivered through Amazon CloudFront (a content delivery network). When your browser requests a page, AWS infrastructure may automatically log:

  • IP address — needed to route the webpage to your browser
  • Timestamp — when the request was made
  • User-agent string — your browser and operating system
  • Requested URL — which page you visited

These are standard web server logs managed by AWS. We do not mine, analyze, or cross-reference this data. For details, see the AWS Privacy Notice.

4

Links to Bolt Bunny

Internet Speed Test is part of the Bolt Bunny family of speed testing tools. Our site contains links to Bolt Bunny, which offers advanced features like test history, AIM Score analysis, and Pro plans.

When you follow a link to boltbunny.app, you leave this site and become subject to Bolt Bunny's Privacy Policy. Bolt Bunny is a separate application with its own data practices, including account registration and payment processing.

5

No Tracking Promise

We believe running a quick internet speed test should not cost you your privacy:

  • No third-party advertising trackers or beacons
  • No data sold, rented, or shared with ISPs or data brokers
  • No browser fingerprinting or device identification
  • No email collection or marketing lists
6

Children's Privacy

Because Internet Speed Test does not collect any personal information, there are no special privacy concerns for users of any age. The site is safe for all visitors, including children under 13.

7

Updates to This Policy

If we change how this site works in a way that affects your privacy, we will update this policy and revise the "Last Updated" date above.
